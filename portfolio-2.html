Aviation CET Official

aviationcetofficial.in

A full-featured exam registration platform for Aviation CET, enabling students to register, authenticate, fill forms, and complete payments seamlessly for aviation entrance examinations.

We developed a comprehensive exam registration platform for Aviation CET Official that provides complete information about aviation entrance examinations, courses, eligibility criteria, and syllabus. The platform enables students to register, authenticate, and complete the entire exam application process online.

The client needed a secure, user-friendly platform that could handle student registrations, authentication, form submissions, payment processing, and automated email notifications. The system also required PDF generation for admit cards and receipts, with reliable deployment on Hostinger.

We built a modern React-based frontend with Material UI for a polished user experience, backed by a robust Node.js and Express server. We implemented secure authentication, integrated a payment gateway for fee collection, used Nodemailer for email automation, and PDFJS for generating downloadable documents. The application was deployed on Hostinger for reliable hosting.
