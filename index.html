Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What does Skydome Aviation's Tech Branch do?

We are the dedicated IT and Software division of Skydome Aviation. We specialize in building custom websites, student portals (like Aviation CET), learning management systems, and tailored enterprise web applications.

What do I need to get started?

To get started, simply share your project requirements, goals, and any reference materials. Our team will guide you through the entire process and outline the best approach for your project.

What level of customization do you offer?

We provide complete customization, including layout structure, color schemes, typography, functionality, and content sections—ensuring the website aligns perfectly with your brand identity.

What industries do you specialize in?

While our core expertise lies in aviation software, exam registration portals, and educational training systems, we also design and build custom web applications for general businesses, e-commerce, and corporate clients.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes. We offer complimentary support for 30 days after launch, along with flexible Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) for ongoing updates, enhancements, and technical support.

Can you redesign or improve our existing platform?

Absolutely. Whether you need a UI/UX refresh, performance optimization, or new feature development, we specialize in enhancing and modernizing existing websites and platforms.
